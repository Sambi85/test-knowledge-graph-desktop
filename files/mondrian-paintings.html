Pieter Cornelis Mondriaan, known after 1911 as Piet Mondrian, was a Dutch painter and art theoretician who is regarded as one of the greatest artists of the 20th century. Wikipedia
Born: March 7, 1872, Amersfoort, Netherlands
Died: February 1, 1944 (age 71 years), New York, NY
Periods: De Stijl, Modern art, Cubism, Impressionism, Expressionism, Modernism, Abstract art
Influenced by: Pablo Picasso, Theo van Doesburg, Georges Braque
Nationality: Dutch
Siblings: Louis Mondrian, Willem Frederik Mondrian, Carel Mondrian
